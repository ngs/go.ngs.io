freecal

A command-line tool to find free time slots in your Google Calendar.

Install:
go install go.ngs.io/freecal@latest

Source: github.com/ngs/freecal
Documentation: pkg.go.dev

Features:

  • OAuth2 authentication with Google Calendar
  • Find free time slots during business hours
  • Configurable working hours and timezone
  • Markdown output format
